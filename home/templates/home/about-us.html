About us

Created in 2019, Vespre is a tee-shirt store with designs reminiscent of sunsets. The word Vespre - Sunset in Catalan - serves as vehicle and inspiration for the designer and owner to create designs based on his main intetests. Ranging from world landmarks, inspiring settings and pop culture references, we're sure you will find something for you!

All our tees are made with just cotton. Well, not just cotton, one of the highest quality cotton you can find! Built with open-end yarn, its tubular design make it so it will endure even the harshest wearing and washing conditions. Vespre tees taped neck and shoulders and double seamed sleeves and bottom make our tees one of the most durable you can find in the market!

Be sure to shoot out an email to us at info@vespre-store.com with any questions you may have!
