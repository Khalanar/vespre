About our tees

Vespre tees are built with just cotton. Well, not just cotton, the most high quality cotton. Built with open-end yarn, its tubular design make it so it will endure even the harshest wearing and washing conditions. Vespre tees taped neck and shoulders and double seamed sleeves and bottom make our tees one of the most durable you can find in the market!

All designs are insipred by the word Vespre (meaning sunset in Catalan) and created by Pablo S. and include a collection of the artist's main intetests. Ranging from world landmarks, inspiring settings and to pop culture references. We're sure you'll find something for you!
